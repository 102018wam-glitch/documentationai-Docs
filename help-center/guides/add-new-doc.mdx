description: Quick tutorial for adding docs to Валерий Медведев's project space.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Go to "My Projects" and choose your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Document'">
    Use the 'Add Document' button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter content">
    Add title and write your documentation in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click 'Save'—your doc is now part of your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
